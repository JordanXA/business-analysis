Green Motors - FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is our goal?

At Green Motors, our goal is to provide you with eco-friendly
motorcycles that aim to reduce the harmful impact on the
environment that come from alternative fuel vehicles.

How do I charge my motorycle?

You can charge your bike by using the provided charger and connecting to
any household outlet for an overnight charge. For a faster charge,
you can visit any charging station and get a full charge in an hour.

What happens if my battery goes bad?

If you feel that your bike is getting less miles with each charge you might
need a new battery. We will offer 8 years of warranty on your vehicles battery
in order to make sure your vehicle is mainting normal performance.

Can I test drive any of your motorycles?

Just like any new vehicle you can make an appointment at any dealership
that offers Green Motors branded vehicles and give them a test
drive before you commit to the purchase.

How does Green Motors help the environment?

Because our motorcycles run on electricity that means our bikes have no tailpipe emission.
That means that no fumes from gasoline are released and helps to reduce air pollution.

Are electric motorcycles better than gas motorcycles?

Electric motorcycles are great for beginners because of the lack of a clutch, it makes them
perfect for beginners. Electric motorycles also require less maintanence than gas motorcycles.
